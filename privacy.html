Privacy Policy

We collect personal information from you, including information about your:

  • - name
  • - contact information
  • - interactions with us

We collect your personal information in order to:

  • - facilitate advanced booking and inform you about the restaurant's new offers, services and events
  • - collect analytical information about how the website is used to allow us improve the user experience

Providing some information is optional. If you choose not to enter name and contact information , we'll be unable to reserve a table booking and updates about the restaurant new offers and services.

If you choose not to enter the ability to store cookies in your web browsers we'll use analytics to improve our website and visitors experience.

We keep your information safe by storing it in encrypted file and only allowing authorized staff to access it.

You have the right to ask for a copy of any personal information we hold about you, and to ask for it to be corrected if you think it is wrong. If you’d like to ask for a copy of your information, or to have it corrected, please contact us at admin@thehaukai.co.nz, or call us at 09-4014019. Alternatively, you can write to us and send to 1 Island Street, Kerikeri, Bay of Islands.
